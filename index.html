Why should you start practicing yoga?

Yoga is your ally in taking care of your health. Yoga act as a gentlemassage of our internal organs and invigorate the entire body, strengthens the immune system and improves overall well-being.

Join us to learn and practise astanga yoga. We're a supportive community where everyone is welcome - from beginners to those with an practice.

Sleep improvement

Rest and relaxation invariably lead to healthy sleep. Yoga will teach you to switch off from problems, to immerse youself in a calm state.

Posture correction

The back will become straighter, posture will improve. the chest will straighten the shoulder well, the stoop will go away.

Muscles and bones

Yoga develops muscle strength and endurance. While you are practicing yoga, your muscles grow and your bones and ligaments strengthen.

Yoga studio Astanga

We are an astanga yoga school that tries to teach in a traditional way so students can progress at their own pace. We offer quality, compassionate teaching. We try to ensure everyone feels welcome as we feel the idea of a supportive community is very important. We usually work with hands-on adjustment: if you feel uncomfortable with this, just let us know: no question or judgement.

Our shala is named 'Dharma Shala' inspired by the story of an ancient Indian king called Ashoka who built many 'dharma shalas' to support wandering yogis. We hope our dharma shala will be a supportive place for you.

Mysore self-practice

At Astanga Yoga London we teach astanga yoga in a tradiional method known as 'Mysore self-practice', which originated in Mysore, South India. Students are taught individually within a group environment and practise at their own pace with one-to-one advice and sometimes adjusments given by the teacher.

Working in this way allows you to focus on your breath and encourages a meditative quality to your practice. Complete newbies are always welcome - join us for more information on how to get Started and what to expect.
